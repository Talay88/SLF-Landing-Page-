what makes a good leader?

This is the question SLF attempts to answer at our annual SOAR event. SOAR encourages people of all ages and backgrounds to become leaders in their local communities. We equip attendees with the problem solving skills and key strategies they need to be great leaders.

There is no pervasive agreement as to what skills and attributes make an effective leader. Ideally, a leader always strives to maintain a balance between meeting the needs of the organization and those of the followers. Leadership is a complex process that combines ethics and relationships with decisive action. Depending on the environment, a leader may use any combination of styles to achieve success.

Different leadership approaches are needed in various settings and cultures. Each individual in an organization is a leader in their own right. Everyone contributes something meaningful to an organization. Without follower's continued participation in the leadership process, the entire system falls apart. There are multiple approaches and facets to leadership that vary significantly upon many different factors.

what is SOAR?

SOAR which stands for Strong, Orgignal, Adaptable and Resilient, emphasizes the four critical principles of great leadership. At SLF, we teach emerging leaders how to utilize these skills and become leaders in their local communities. This annual event includes workshops, influential speakers from many industries, and opportunities to connect and network with other professionals.
