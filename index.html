SLF is hosting its annual SOAR event at the Los Angeles Convention Center on October 4, 2025. The event will feature inspiring speakers, interactive workshops, and networking opportunities.

about the event

SOAR represents the four key components of successful leadership: strong, original, adaptable, and resilient. With problem solving skills and key strategies, leaders can help organizations and followers reach their full potential.

This annual event encourages people of all ages and backgrounds to become leaders in their local communities. Best of all, this event is free! Register today and join us as we discuss what it means to be a great leader. SOAR high and learn what your capable of!
